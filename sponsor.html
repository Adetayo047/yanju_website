Common Questions

Everything you need to know about sponsoring a child.

How is my money spent? expand_more
Approximately 85% of your donation goes directly to the child's needs—school fees, supplies, health programs, and nutritional support. The remaining 15% covers essential local staff support and monitoring to ensure your child is thriving and safe.
Can I write letters to my child? expand_more
Yes! We encourage communication. You can send letters and photos through our secure online portal. Our local team assists children who are still learning to read and write, and you will receive at least two personal updates or letters from your child each year.
Is sponsorship a long-term commitment? expand_more
Sponsorship is most effective when it's a long-term relationship, ideally lasting through the child's education. However, we understand that circumstances change. You can cancel your sponsorship at any time, and we will work immediately to find a new sponsor so the child's support is never interrupted.
