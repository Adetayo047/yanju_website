How can we help?

Find answers to common questions or chat with our AI assistant to learn more about our mentorship, STEM, and scholarship programs.

quiz General Queries

How do I apply for a scholarship? expand_more
To apply for a scholarship, please visit our Programs page and select the 'Scholarships' section. Applications open twice a year in January and July. You will need to provide academic transcripts, a letter of recommendation, and a personal essay detailing your aspirations.
Where do my donations go? expand_more
100% of public donations go directly to program funding. This includes STEM workshop materials, tuition fees for scholarship recipients, and training for our volunteer mentors. Administrative costs are covered separately by our corporate partners.
Can I volunteer from overseas? expand_more
Yes! We have several virtual mentorship tracks where international professionals can guide students via video calls and collaborative online tools. We also welcome remote help with technical tasks like curriculum design and web development.
Is the foundation registered? expand_more
Yanju Foundation is a fully registered non-profit organization with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC). We maintain transparency through annual reports published on our website every December.
